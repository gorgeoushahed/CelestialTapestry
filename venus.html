Venus


Venus is the second planet from the sun, and it's often referred to as the Earth's sister planet due to their similar sizes and compositions. However, that's where the similarities end. Venus is a world of extremes, with surface temperatures hot enough to melt lead and an atmosphere thick with toxic gases. It's also the brightest object in the sky after the sun and moon, earning it the nickname "the morning star" or "the evening star." Despite its inhospitable nature, Venus has long captivated humans with its enigmatic beauty. It's a world of mystery and intrigue, and we're only just beginning to unlock its secrets.
